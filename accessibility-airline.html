    Overview of the project

    The U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT) issued a rule requiring Airlines' websites to comply to level AA of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines version 2.0 (WCAG v2.0) by the end of 2015. I have work to achieve this goal with Amadeus on the e-Retail booking system, which is used by over a hundred airlines, as well as directly with various airlines such as Japan Airlines, Avianca or China Southern Airlines.

    My role

    As an intern at Amadeus, and later as a UX consultant at System Concepts, I conducted:

    • Accessibility audits.
    • Accessibility testing.
    • Training, awareness.
