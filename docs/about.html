About me


Hej hej! I'm Sophie, a Product Designer based in Stockholm.

I strive to contribute to products that bring value to people's life, and that work for all people.

I'm pretty obsessed with research, data, and understanding people and problems. And I love finding the best ways to communicate complex information to people, be it the users I'm designing for or the co-workers I'm sharing my work with.

My colleagues describe me as kind, honest, wise, and a great source for feedback.


I studied Software Engineering at Telecom Sud Paris and Human-Computer-Interaction at Blekinge Tekniska Högskola. I’ve worked at H&M, Travel company Thomas Cook, UX consultancy System Concepts, and technology provider Amadeus.


Outside of work, I enjoy:

  • spending time with my husband Alexis and my 18 months old son Nolan,
  • playing board games (with lots of strategy, some collaboration, and no place for luck!),
  • reading a lot,
  • listening to my favourite Podcasts.
