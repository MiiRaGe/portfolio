Hiya! I'm Sophie, a Product Designer based in Stockholm. I'm originally from the north-east of France, and I lived in the UK for a while before settling in Sweden.

I love figuring out how people work and think, and turning that into products that are intuitive, and genuinely useful. Whether I'm designing an experience or collaborating with teams, I'm obsessed with simplicity and efficiency.

When I'm not working, I'm usually with my husband and our two sons (7 and 4), playing a strategy board game or reading way too much (I'm in two book clubs, where "gentle" discussions get surprisingly heated). And of course, I love listening to podcasts!

Background

I'm currently a Lead Product Designer at Acast, building products for content creators and sellers, and driving cross-team initiatives to improve the experience across our product offering.

I've worked at H&M, travel company Thomas Cook, UX consultancy System Concepts, and technology provider Amadeus.

I studied Software Engineering at Telecom SudParis and Human-Computer Interaction at Blekinge Tekniska Högskola.
