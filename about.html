Hello :)

I'm Sophie Durand, a UX Specialist currently based in London.

I have worked at:

  • Travel company Thomas Cook, where I help to ensure the experience of finding and booking a holiday is seamless.
  • London-based consultancy System Concepts, where I improved the usability and accessibility of a range of products for companies such as the BBC, Vodafone and Sainsbury's.
  • Technology provider Amadeus, where I worked on making user-friendly software for travel professionals, as well as sites and apps for airlines.

I hold a degree in Software Engineering from Telecom Sud Paris, with a specialisation in Human-Computer-Interaction. During my studies I spend some time at the Blekinge Institute of Technology in Sweden.

Outside of work, I like:

  • reading (You can check out what I've been reading on Goodreads),
  • playing board games,
  • baking.
