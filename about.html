    • Hello world :)

    I'm Sophie Durand. I'm a Senior UX Researcher at Thomas Cook in London.
    I help to understand what users do on websites and applications, why they do it and how to make sure they enjoy their experience!

    I hold a degree in Software Engineering from Telecom Sud Paris, with a specialisation in Human-Computer-Interaction.

    I have worked at:

    • Amadeus, making great software for travel professionals and booking websites for airlines.
    • London-based consultancy System Concepts, helping to improve the usability and accessibility of products for companies such as the BBC, Vodafone and Sainsbury's.
    • Travel company Thomas Cook, improving a multi-market travel website.

    Outside of work, I like reading (all kinds of books! You can check out what I've been reading on Goodreads), playing board games and baking.
